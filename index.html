North Model United Nations

Burnaby’s Premiere day Conference

-
22 May 2021

 

Dearest delegates and members of the NorthMUN community,

My name is Kathy Wu and it is my distinct pleasure to welcome you to the third iteration of North Model United Nations set to be held on Saturday 22 May 2021. This year, our world has faced many challenges, and, on behalf of the entire Secretariat team at NorthMUN, we offer our deepest sympathies to those affected by these unforeseen circumstances. 

After careful consideration of the impact of COVID-19 in British Columbia and around the world, the NorthMUN Secretariat team has made the difficult, yet exciting, decision to move entirely online in order to ensure the wellbeing of our Secretariat, staff, delegates, and volunteers. 

Whilst NorthMUN 2021 will not look exactly as the Secretariat had originally hoped, we strive to deliver the same level of engagement and thought-provoking conversation that an in-person experience would provide. With one of NorthMUN’s core pillars being accessibility, a virtual conference will surely allow us to appeal to an international audience—leading to diverse committees rich in opinions. As well, we are excited to offer lower prices and smaller, more intimate committees to provide an exceptional experience in the virtual format. We have no doubt that our virtual model will allow us to further emphasise our mission of inspiring diplomacy and leadership through our accessible conference. 

Through the hard work and dedication of our staff and Secretariat members, we guarantee that our conference will be engaging, memorable, and, most importantly, safe. If any questions arise, please contact me at sg@northmun.com or feel free to take a look at our frequently asked questions page. Thank you for joining us this year, and we sincerely welcome you to NorthMUN 2021!

Warmest regards,

 
 

Kathy Wu
Secretary-General
